About Me

Hi there, My name is Josh Payne. First of all thank you for visiting my portfolio website. I hope to show you the best of my abilities at this site. I am an aspiring full stack web developer looking to create beatiful websites that are easy to navigate, aesthetically pleasing, and informative. I have wanted to learn how to code since I was thirteen when my brother and friends would get together and host LAN parties. It fascinated me that there were people out there who could make this possible through the power of code. Until recently I lacked the means and opportunity to pursue this dream but I am so glad to be on this journey and am looking forward to one day be able to do this full time.
